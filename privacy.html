Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how 22stagioni ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our social media automation application.

1. What We Collect

Our application accesses the following data solely to publish content on your behalf:

  • TikTok account identifier and access tokens, used exclusively to upload and publish videos to your TikTok account.
  • Basic profile information returned by the TikTok API during authentication.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok API on your behalf.
  • Upload and publish video content to your TikTok account.
  • Refresh access tokens to maintain uninterrupted service.

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored in a private, self-hosted database (Supabase) accessible only to the application owner. No data is stored on third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

4. Data Retention

Tokens and account data are retained for as long as the integration is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke access at any time by disconnecting the app from your TikTok account settings at tiktok.com/settings/connected-apps.
